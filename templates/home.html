MEDmine

This app is used to find genes that are fairly new and aren't a part of Radboud's genpanels yet.

Using the search feature:

  1. Enter your desired search term to the term field.
  2. Select the field you want to search.
  3. Add to the query by selecting one of the three keywords: 'AND', 'OR' or 'NOT'.
  4. Enter a date to find only articles published since the date.
  5. Exclude a genpanels if desired.
  6. Enter your email.
  7. Click the Search button.

Your search will now commence, if there is a sizable amount of found articles this could take a minute or two.

Make sure to keep the browser window open!

How to interpret the results:

  1. On the top of page a UUID will be displayed, with this UUID you'll be able to find your results again.
  2. Beneath the UUID you will find the query used for your search, the selected genpanel and the selected date.
  3. Underneath the search information, you will be able to see your results.
    • The left column displays newly found genes, together with their gene id, how often they're found in an article, and the article ID of the article they were found in.
    • The right column will display the disease name, together with their MESH id, how often they're found in an article and the article id of the article they're found in.
  4. You can click on either the genes or diseases to highlight the ones found in the same article.

How to use the history feature:

  1. Enter your UUID to find your results.
  2. You will be send back to the results page which will show the results corresponding to the submitted UUID.

Underneath the search button you will find the UUIDs of your five most recent searches.
